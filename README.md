# HR-Data-Analytics-Dashboad

An Interactive Data Visualization to understand the attrition rate of employees across different departments by HR.

# Project Summary - Employee Attrition Rate Department wise HR Data Analytics Dashboard

This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Tableau, designed to help HR Teams & Management, to monitor workforce health, attrtion trends, and employee satisfaction at a glance. The dashboard converts raw HR data into actionable insights through intuitive KPIs, charts and filter.

# Objective

To objective is to help HR leaders to analyse the employee attrition trends, identify high-risk groups and analyse workforce trends across gender, age, department, education and job sarisfaction.

# Key Features of the Dashboard:-

**1. KPI Cards**
The top section provides a high level overview using KPI Cards

* **Total Employees:** 1470
* **Attrition Count:** 237
* **Attrition Rate:** 16.12%
* **Active Employees:** 1,233
* **Average Age:** 37

**Purpose:** Enables leadership to instantly assess overall workforce health and attrition severity.

**2. Attrition by Gender**
A horizontal bar visual shows:

* **Male Attrition:** 150
* **Female Attrition:** 87

**Insight:** Male employees shows a higher attrtion count as compared to female employees indicating a need fo targeted engagement or policy review.

**3.Department-wise Attrtion**
A pie chart breaks attrtion across:

* **R&D - 56.12%**
* **Sales - 38.82%**
* **HR - 5.06%**

**Insight:** R&D experiences the highest attrition, making it a critical focus area for retention efforts.

**4.Employee Distribution by Age Group**
A histogram visual shows employee count by age bins:

* Majority of employees fall between **27-36 years**.
* Lower workforce presence beyond **50+ age group**

**Value:** Helps HR aligh workforce planning, hiring and succession strategies.

**4.Job Satisfaction by Job Role**
A matrix heatmap helps in analyzing the satisfaction levels (1-4) across roles such as:

* Sales Executive
* Research Scientist
* Laboratory Technician
* Manager

**Insight:** Roles with lower satisfaction scores corealte with higher attrition, signaling areas needing policy intervention.

**6.Education Field-wise Attrition
A bar chart highlights attrition by educational background:

* **Life Sciences:** 89
* **Medical:** 63
* **Marketing:** 35
* **Technical Degree:** 32

**Insight:** Employees from Life Scinces and Medical fields show higher attrtition, helping HR leaders tailor career paths and learning programs.

**7. Attrition Rate by Gender & Age Group**
Donut charts display attrition across age groups segmented by gender:

* Highest attrition observed in **25-34 age group.**
* Male attrition remains consistently higher across most age groups.

**Value:** Supports demographic-specific retention initiatives.

**8. Interactive Filters**

* **Education Filter.**
* Gender-base color coding.

**Benefit:** Enables the stakeholders to explore attrition patterns across different employee segments.

# Summary

This HR Analytics Dashboard effectively transforms raw HR Data into meaningful insights using KPI cards, bar charts, pie charts, heatmaps, and interactive filters. It provides a holistic view of employee attrition and workforce dynamics, empowering HR teams to reduce attrition, improve job satisfaction, and enhance worforce stability.
